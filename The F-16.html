The F-16

The F-16 Fighting Falcon is a single-engine, multirole fighter aircraft developed by General Dynamics and Lockheed Martin. It's known for its maneuverability and versatility, used in both air-to-air and ground attack missions

General:

  • Type: Multirole fighter, air superiority fighter
  • Manufacture: General Dynamic, Lockheed Martin
  • Crew: F-16C One; F-16D: One or Two

    • Dimensions:

  • Wingspan: 32 feet, 8 inches (9.8 meters)
  • Length: 49 feet, 5 inches (14.8 meters)
  • Height: 16 feet (4.8 meters)

    • Weights:

  • Empty Weight: 18,820 lbs
  • Gross Weight: 42,300 lbs
  • Maximum Takeoff Weight: 37,500 pounds (16,875 kilograms)

    • Performance:

  • Top Speed: 1,500 mph (Mach 2 at altitude)
  • Service Ceiling: 50,000 feet plus
  • Range: Over 2,002 miles ferry range (1,740 nautical miles)

    • Powerplant:

  • Engine: Pratt & Whitney F100-PW-200/220/229 or General Electric F110-GE-100/129
  • Thrust: 27,000 pounds

    • Armament:

  • Cannon: One M-61A1 20mm multibarrel with 500 rounds
  • Air-to-Air Missiles: Can carry up to six air-to-air missiles (e.g,AIM-9,AIM-120)
  • External Load: Up to 12,00 lbs of external load

    • Other:

  • Fuel Capacity: 7,000 pounds internal, with two external tanks, a typical capacity of 12,000 pounds
  • Payload: Can carry a variety of munitions, including bombs, missiles and external fuel tanks

    • Key avionics systems:

  • Navigation systems: Enhanced Global Positioning and Intertial Navigation System (EGI) provide accurate position and steering information
  • Radar: The F-16 utilizes radar systems like the AN/APG-68 or AN/APG-82 to detect, track and identify targets and to operate in hostile electronic warfare (EW) enviroments
  • Communication system: UHF and VHF radio, along with ans instrument landing system enable communicaton and navigation
  • Electronic warfare: The aircraft features warning systems and countermeasure pods to counter electronic threats
  • Flight control and data systems: Flight computers, fly-by-wire vontrols and other systems mnage the aircraft flight characteristics and data transmission according to Fly a jet fighter
  • Mission-specific avionics: Depnding on the varient and mission, the F-16 may also inclde systems like advanced targeting pods, according to Lockheed Martin and data links

  • F-16C Block 50: This version includes Honeywell H-423 Ring Laser Gyro Inertial Navigation Systems, a GPS reciver and am upgraded programmable display generator
