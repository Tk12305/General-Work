The M1 Abram

The M1 Abrams is a US Main Battle Tank (MBT) with key specifications including a multi-fuel gas turbine engine, a high-speed suspension, and a crew of four

Engine and Performance:

Engine:Multi=fuel gas turbine engine producing 1,500 hp

Maximun Speed:42 mph (67 km/h) on road, 30 mph (48 km/h) cross-country

Operational Range:265 miles (426 km) road, 93-124 miles (150-200 km) cross-country

Suspension:Torsion bar suspension with rotary shock absorbers

Armament:

Main Gun:120mm smoothbore cannon

Machine Guns:7.62mm M420 machine gun, 12.7mm M2HB machine gun

Ammunition:Various types of ammunition for the main gun and machine guns

Armor and Protection:

Chobham Composite Armor:A highly effective composite armor providing protection against various threats

Thickness:Variable thickness depenfing on the location on the tank, with the front of the hull being the thickest

Other Specifications:

Crew:4 (Commander,Gunner,Loader,Driver)

Weight:68 tons

Fuel Capacity:Approximatley 500 US gallons

Dimensions:Length:9.7 m, Width:3.7 m, Height: 2.4 m
