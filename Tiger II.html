The Tiger II

The Sd.Kfz. 182, also known as the Panzerkampfwagen VI Ausf. B or Tiger II, was a heavy German tank used in World War II

Key Specifications:

  • Weight: 69.8 tons
  • Length: 10.3 m
  • Width: 3.76 m
  • Height: 3.09 m
  • Engine: Maybach HL230 P30 V12 petrol engine (same as Tiger I)
  • Speed: 26 kph
  • Crew: 5 (commander, gunner, loader, driver, radio operator)
  • Armament: 88 mm KwK 43 L/71 gun, two 7.92mm MG34 machine guns
  • Armor: 180 mm
  • Production: 489 units, from January 1944 to March 1945
