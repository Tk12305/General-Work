Rockwell B1B Lancer

The Rockwell B1B Lancer is a supersonic variable-sweep wing, heavy bomber used by the United States Air Force.

Length - 44.5 Meters

Height - 10.4 Meters

Wingspan - 41.8 Meters Wings extended forward - 24.1 Meters Wings swept aft

Weight - Empty Weight - 86,183 Kilograms - Maximum Takeoff weight - 216,634 Kilograms

Fuel Capacity - 120,326 Kilograms

Payload - 34,019 Kilograms

Engine - Four General Eletric F101-GE-102 turbofan engines with afterburner

Speed - 900 MPH+ (Mach 1.2)

Crew - Four people
