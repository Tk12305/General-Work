The English Electric lightning

The English Electric Lightning is a supersonic interceptor aircraft

Dimensions and weight:

  • Length: 55 ft 3 in (16.84 m)
  • Wingspan: 34 ft 10 in (10.62 m)
  • Height: 19 ft 7 in (5.97 m)
  • Empty Weight: 28,036 lbs (12,717 kg)
  • Maximum Take-off Weight (MTOW): 41,991 lbs (19,047 kg)

    • Performance:

  • Maximum Speed: Mach 2.27 (2,414 km/h, 1,500 mph) at 40,000 ft
  • Sevice ceiling: 60,00 ft (18,000 m)
  • Maximum Range: 800 miles (1,290 km) with ventral tanks
  • Intial Rate of Climb: 50,000 ft per minute

    • Engines:

  • Powerplant: Two Rolls-Royce Avon Mk.302 afterburning turbojet engines
  • thrust (dry): 28.3 kN (12,600 lbf) per engine
  • Thrust (with reheat): 36.6 kN (16,300 lbf) per engine

    • Armament:

  • The Lightning F.6 had two 30mm Aden cannons, and hard points for air-to-air missiles
  • Other varients carried a range of weaponry, including rockets and bombs
