The Grumman F14 Tomcat

The Grumman F-14 Tomcat is a variable-sweep twin-engine, supersonic, two-seat, all-weather naval fighter jet designed for air superiority

Dimensions:

  • Length: 62 ft 9 in (19.1 m)
  • Height: 16 ft (4.88 m)
  • Wingspan: 64 ft (19.55 m) (unswept) / 38 ft (11.58 m) (swept)

    • Weight:

  • Empty: 40,104 lb (19,838 kg)
  • loaded: 61,000 lb (27,700 kg)
  • Maxi. Takeoff: 74,350 lb (33,720 kg)

    • Performance:

  • Maximum Speed: Mach 2.34 (1,544 mph)
  • Combat Range: 500 nmi (575 mi, 926 km)
  • Ferry Range: 1,600 nmi (1,840 mi, 2,960 km)
  • Cruising Speed: 550 mph
  • Climb Rate: >45,000 ft/min (229 m/s)
  • Sevice Ceiling: 50,000+ ft (15,200 mi)

    • Powerplant:

  • Two Pratt & Whitney TF30-P412A engines
  • F-14B/D use F110-GE-400 engines

    • Crew:

  • Two (pilot and radar intercept officer (RIO))

    • Weaponry:

  • One M61 Vulcan 20mm cannon
  • Air-to-air missiles: AIM-54 Phoenix, AIM-7 Sparrow, AIM-9 Sidewinder
  • Bombs: GBU-10, GBU-12, GBU-16, GBU-24, GBU-24E Paveway I/II/III, GBU-31, GBU-38 JDAM, MK-29 Rockeye II, MK-82, MK-83, MK-84
