Boeing F/A-18E/F Super-Hornet

The F/A-18E/F Super Hornet is a powerful, multi-role fighter aircraft

General:

Manufacturer:Boeing

Type:Multi-role fighter aircraft

Crew:2 (two-seat variant) or 1 (single-seat varient)

Dimensions:

Length:18.48 Meters

Wingspan:13.68 Meters

Height:4.88 Metres

Weight:

Empty Weight:14,288 Kilograms

Maximum Takeoff Weight:29,938 Kilograms

Maximum Carrier landing Weight:19,459 Kilograms

Performance:

Maximum Speed:Mach 1.8+ (apporximately 1,400 mph)

Ceiling:50,000+ feet

Range:1,253 miles

Powerplant:

Engines:2 x General Eletric F414-GE-400 turbofan engines

Thrust:Each engine provides 22,000 lbs of static thrust

Other Notable Features:

Armament:Can carry a variety of air-to-air and air-to-surface missiles, bombs, and other ordnance

Radar:Features a powerful radar system, including the APG-79 active electronically scanned array (AESA) radar

Stealth Capabilites:While not a true stealth fighter, the Super Hornet has a smaller radar cross-section (RCS) than its predecessor, the F/A-18 Hornet
