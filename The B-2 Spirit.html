The B-2 Spirit

The B-2 Spirit is a long-range, stealth bomber capable of carrying both conventional and nuclear weapons

Dimensions and Weight:

  • Wingspan: 172 feet (52.12 meters)
  • Length: 69 feet (20.9 meters)
  • Height: 17 feet (5.1 meters)
  • Empty Weight: 160,000 pounds (72,575 kilograms)
  • Maximum Takeoff Weight: 336,500 pounds (152,634 kilograms)

    • Engine and Performance:

  • Power Plant: Four General Electric F118-GE-100 turbofans
  • Thrust: 17,300 pounds each engine
  • Fuel Capacity: 167,000 pounds (75750 kilograms)
  • Speed: High subsonic
  • Payload: 40,000 pounds (18,144 kilograms)
  • Range: Intercontinetal
  • Ceiling: 50,000 feet (15,240 meters)

    • Armament:

  • Nuclear: Can carry a variety of nuclaer weapons
  • Convential: Can carry a variety of covential weapons including GBU-31/38/39 JDAMs, Mk 82/84 bombs and others

    • Other:

  • Unit Cost: Approximatley $1.157 billion (fiscal 1998 contant dollars)
  • Initial Operating Capability: April 1997
