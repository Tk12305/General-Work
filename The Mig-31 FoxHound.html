The Mig-31 FoxHound

Dimensions:

Length: 22.62 m (74 Ft 3 In)

Wingspan: 13.46 m (44 Ft 2 In)

Height: 6.46 m (21 Ft 2 In)

Wingspan: 61.6 m2 (663 Sq Ft)

Weight:

Empty Weight: 21,820 kg (48,105 lb)

Gross Weight: 41,000 Kg (90,390 lb)

Max Takeoff Weight: 46,200 Kg (101,854 lb)

Performance:

Engines: 2 x Solovie D-30F6 afterburning Turbofan Engines

Thrust: 93 kN (21,000 lbf) dry, 152 kN (34,000 lbf) with Afterburner

Maximum Speed: Mach 2.83

Service Ceiling: 20,600 m (67,600 Ft)

Rate of Climb: 208 m/s

Maximum G-Load: 5

Armament:

Built-in Gun: GSh-6-23M (30mm) 23mm Gatling Gun

Air-to-air missiles: Typically carries a mix of R-33E Long-range, R-40TD1 Medium-range, and R-60MK short-range missiles

Other Notable features:

Features an advanced Zaslon phased-array radar system

Can operate in conjunction with other Mig-31s for expanded coverage through digital datalink sharing

Airframe construction includes arc-welded nickle steel, light metal alloy, titanium and composites
